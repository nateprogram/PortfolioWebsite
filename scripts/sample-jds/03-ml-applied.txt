Applied ML Engineer, New Grad

We're hiring new-grad Applied ML engineers to ship production ML systems at a B2B AI company (Series B, 80 people, profitable). You'll work end-to-end on ML features: data pipelines, training, evaluation, deployment, monitoring. Less research, more shipping.

The work
- Build data pipelines that pull from PostgreSQL, S3, and partner APIs into our training pipeline.
- Train and evaluate models (mostly classical ML + transformer fine-tuning). PyTorch is our default.
- Productionize models behind FastAPI services. Onnx export, Triton serving where latency matters.
- Build evaluation harnesses. Most of our model improvements come from better evals, not better architectures.
- Work with product engineers in TypeScript/Next.js to ship ML features to real users.

Required
- BS in CS, Math, Statistics, or equivalent by Summer 2026.
- Strong Python. Comfort with NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn, PyTorch.
- Understanding of ML fundamentals: train/val/test splits, overfitting, calibration, common eval metrics.
- A real project: a Kaggle medal, a published paper, a non-trivial ML side project shipped to users, or relevant research/internship work.
- Comfort with SQL, Git, Linux.

Nice to have
- LLM/RAG experience (prompt engineering, embeddings, vector databases).
- Production ML experience (serving, monitoring, A/B testing).
- TypeScript / web stack so you can ship a full feature without a handoff.

About
SF or NYC. Hybrid. Equity-heavy comp at the new-grad level. Six interns from the last two cycles have converted to full-time at this level.
