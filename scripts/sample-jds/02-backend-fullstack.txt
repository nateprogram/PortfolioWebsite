New Grad Software Engineer, Platform

Stripe-like fintech, Series C, 220 people. We are looking for new-grad engineers to join the Platform team. You'll work on the internal services that power our payment APIs, including idempotency infrastructure, webhook delivery, async job processing, and our developer-facing CLI.

What you'll do
- Ship code to production within your first two weeks. Most engineers ship their first PR on day three.
- Design and implement backend services in TypeScript (Node.js, Next.js) backed by PostgreSQL and Redis.
- Build and maintain REST APIs consumed by internal teams and external partners.
- Write tests. Real ones. We don't merge code without them.
- On-call rotation begins after month four; mentored shadowing for the first three months.

What we want
- Graduating with a BS in CS, CE, or equivalent by Summer 2026.
- Comfortable with TypeScript and at least one other language (Python, Go, Rust, Java).
- Strong fundamentals: data structures, algorithms, complexity analysis.
- Experience with PostgreSQL (or any relational database in real depth).
- Familiarity with Git, GitHub, code review.
- Bonus: prior internship at a software company, contributions to open source, side projects you've shipped to real users.

Tech stack
TypeScript, Node.js, Next.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, AWS (Lambda, S3, RDS, SQS), Vercel for our marketing site, Datadog, Sentry, Stripe API obviously.

Location
San Francisco HQ, hybrid (3 days in office) or remote within the US (PST overlap required).
